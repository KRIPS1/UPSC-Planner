Beginner Strategy — What To Prioritize
  • 📰 Daily newspaper (30 min): The Hindu or Indian Express. Note schemes, bills, committees, judgments — these appear in both Prelims & Mains.
  • ✍️ Writing practice from Day 1: UPSC rewards structured, analytical writing. Start with 150-word answers; graduate to full 250-word answers by Month 3.
  • 🎯 MCQ daily habit: 25–50 MCQs/day. Review every wrong answer — the explanation matters more than the score.
  • 📚 NCERTs first: Classes 6–12 for History, Geography, Polity, Economy, Science. These are the foundation. Don't skip them.
  • ⚠️ Negative marking: Prelims has -0.33 penalty per wrong MCQ. Accuracy > speed in early stages.
  • 🧠 Ethics (GS IV): Unique paper — focus on case studies and moral frameworks. Practice answer writing extensively here.
  • 📝 Essay Paper (250 marks): Hugely underrated. Practice one essay per week. Structure matters as much as content.
